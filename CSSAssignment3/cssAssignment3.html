Places I Would Like to Visit






I would like to visit these six destinations for a variety of reasons. I would first like to visit New Zealand for its picturesque countryside and to see the set where Lord of the Rings was filmed. I would like to visit London to experience the big city life in Europe. Scotland would be a wonderful place to visit because of its beautiful coastline and cliffs. I have always wanted to visit Alaska to see the wild, untamed land of North America. I would like to visit Antarctica not only for its beauty, but also to claim I have been where few people have gone before. Lastly, I want to visit Mount Fuji to see the inside of one of the world's most famous volcanoes.
